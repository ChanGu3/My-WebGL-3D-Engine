Note: These are the current controls for the Editor
Press O: Play
Press P: Stop  // yes this is confusing on accident keeping cause hilarious
Press L: Switch Camera From Scene To Editor Camera (Even During Play)

Controls...,
Press W: forward
Press S: backward
Press A: strafe left
Press D: strafe right
Press C: move down
Press SPACE: move up
Press UP: look up
Press DOWN: look down
Press LEFT: look left
Press RIGHT: look right
Press E: look rotate right
Press Q: look rotate left


For The LightGameScene,

Press E: whenever close enough to a orb clicking them will direct them to the orb in the middle making them spin around with the orb
